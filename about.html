The Gold Standard in Field Services

Forged in Excellence

The name Aunix is built upon 'Au'—the chemical symbol for gold. This isn't just a name; it's our promise. It represents our commitment to being the gold standard in outsourced IT field services for Managed Service Providers across South Africa.

We were established to solve a critical industry challenge: the gap between an MSP's capabilities and their clients' on-site needs. We exist to bridge that gap with unparalleled professionalism and reliability, allowing our partners to expand their reach and deepen their client relationships.

Our Mission

To forge lasting partnerships by empowering MSPs to scale confidently, increase profitability, and deliver exceptional on-site service without the burden of logistical complexities. We handle the groundwork, so you can focus on strategic growth.
