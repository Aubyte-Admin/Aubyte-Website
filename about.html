The Team Behind Your Team

Our Story

Aunix was founded by Bernu Britz after years of working within and alongside Managed Service Providers. We saw a recurring, critical challenge for MSPs of all sizes: the difficulty and high cost of providing fast, reliable, and professional on-site support across a wide geographical area. Projects were delayed, and potential clients were turned away because of logistical constraints.

We built this company to be the perfect solution to that problem. We are not another MSP; we are a dedicated, expert field services team that MSPs can rely on as their own. Our entire business model is built around empowering you to do more.

Our Mission

To empower South African MSPs with exceptional, reliable, and scalable outsourced IT services. We measure our success by the success and growth of our partners. We handle the hands-on work so you can focus on building relationships and recurring revenue.

Our Core Values

  • Partnership: We function as an extension of your team, not a third-party vendor. Your goals are our goals.
  • Reliability: When we accept a job, it gets done right. You can count on us to be there on time and prepared.
  • Professionalism: Every interaction with your client reflects on your brand. Our technicians are courteous, skilled, and professional at all times.
  • Flexibility: We understand that the IT world is dynamic. We adapt to your processes and your clients' unique needs.
