About Aubyte

What’s our story?

At Aubyte, we deliver outsourced IT services to businesses across South Africa. Our mission is simple: to be the trusted partner that businesses rely on for consistent, high-quality on-site IT support—wherever and whenever it's needed.

We were founded to solve a growing challenge in today’s fast-paced business environment: bridging the gap between centralised IT services and on-the-ground support. Whether you're an MSP looking to extend your reach or a business in need of dependable on-site technical services, Aubyte ensures you have a skilled, professional team on your side—without the complexity of managing it yourself.

Our name, inspired by the chemical symbol for gold (Au), reflects our commitment to excellence, reliability, and lasting value. These principles are not just part of our brand—they define how we work, the standards we hold ourselves to, and the results we deliver.
