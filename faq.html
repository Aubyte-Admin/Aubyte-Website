Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact Aubyte for support or inquiries?

The best way to start a conversation is by using the form on our Contact Page or by sending an email directly to info@aubyte.co.za. For urgent matters, existing partners can reach us at the dedicated support line provided during onboarding. We aim to respond to all inquiries within one business day.

What should I do if I have an issue with a service provided?

Your satisfaction is our top priority. If you encounter any issues or are not satisfied with a service performed, please contact your dedicated account manager immediately. We are committed to resolving any problems swiftly and professionally to ensure both you and your client are satisfied with the outcome.

How does the white-label process work?

Our partnership is built on being an invisible extension of your brand. Our technicians arrive on-site in unbranded attire and vehicles. They introduce themselves as representatives of your company and follow your specific operational procedures. All communication and reporting are channeled through you, ensuring your client relationship remains central.

What is your service area?

We operate nationwide across all provinces of South Africa. Our network of field engineers allows us to provide consistent, reliable service whether your client is in a major city or a more remote location.

What are your response times?

Response times are defined in our Service Level Agreement (SLA), which is customized during the onboarding process. We offer various tiers, including standard next-business-day response and expedited 4-hour response for critical emergencies, depending on your needs and client commitments.
