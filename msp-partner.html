The Aunix Partner Programme

Grow Your MSP with a True Partner

Your Strategic Advantage

Our MSP Partner Programme is designed to make you more successful. By handling your on-site needs, we free you to focus on strategy and growing your recurring revenue. We become an invisible extension of your team.

Request a Consultation
  • Increase Your Margins: Reduce travel time, vehicle expenses, and the cost of keeping technicians on standby. Service clients profitably, anywhere in the country.
  • Purely White-Label: We are your invisible team. Our technicians operate under your brand, use your ticketing system, and follow your procedures to ensure a seamless client experience.
  • Expand Your Service Area: Confidently say "yes" to prospective clients outside your current geographical range. Turn nationwide opportunities into profitable contracts.
  • Focus on Your Strengths: Let us handle the physical, time-consuming on-site tasks while your core team focuses on high-value remote support, cybersecurity, and strategic client management.
  • Guaranteed SLAs: We work with you to establish Service Level Agreements that meet your clients' expectations, ensuring fast and reliable response times for all on-site needs.

How It Works: A Simple Process

We've streamlined our process to be as efficient and seamless as possible.

1. Consultation & Onboarding

We start with a call to understand your business, clients, and technical requirements. We establish communication protocols and service level expectations.

2. Service Request

When you need on-site support, you simply send a dispatch request to us through your preferred channel (email, portal, or phone call) with the job details.

3. Dispatch & Execution

We dispatch a qualified technician to the client site. They perform the work professionally under your brand and provide detailed service reports upon completion.
