Policies & Procedures

At Aubyte, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, privacy, and service delivery. Below is a summary of our key policies. Full, detailed policy documents are provided to all partners upon engagement.

Privacy Policy

Aubyte respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. We collect information through our contact form (name, company, email) solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiries and establishing a business relationship. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We implement robust security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access.

Terms of Service (Summary)

Our services are provided to Managed Service Providers (MSPs) under a formal Master Service Agreement (MSA). Key terms include:

  • Scope of Work: All services will be performed as detailed in a mutually agreed-upon Statement of Work (SOW) for each project or dispatch.
  • Confidentiality: We treat all information regarding your business and your clients' businesses with strict confidentiality.
  • White-Label Agreement: All services are performed under your brand. Our technicians act as representatives of your company.
  • Limitation of Liability: Our liability is limited as per the terms outlined in the signed Master Service Agreement.

Cookie Policy

Our website, aubyte.co.za, may use "cookies" to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files placed on your device that help us analyze web traffic and improve our website's functionality. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.
